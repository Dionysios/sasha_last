Casa Joaninha

Lovely detached villa with swimming pool in Guia (central Algarve, Portugal). The house is located in a beautiful and well maintained estate with extensive green areas and gardens. The villa is facing south with plenty of sunshine. It is situated at walking distance to the village of Guia with its facilities of restaurants, banks, pharmacy and supermarkets. The Algarve Shopping (one of the biggest shopping centers of the Algarve) includes many shops, restaurants and cinema. The town Albufeira and some of the best beaches in Algarve are located within a few minutes’ drive. The property is fully fenced. The pool area has a shower, barbecue, covered terrace and comfortable relax area.

Ground Floor

Hall, kitchen, 2 bedrooms, lounge and dining room (with TV) and patio doors to the covered terrace. The kitchen is equipped with fridge/freezer, dishwasher, washing machine, tumble drier, oven, micro, coffee machine, toaster, porcelain and cutlery.

First Floor

Working room (with a baby bed), bedroom with bath room en suite and balcony.
